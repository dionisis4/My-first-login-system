Informations about Silk & Grain

The restaurant Silk & Grain is located in Piccadilly. The Piccadilly Circus is in the center of the famous statue of the winged Cupid. It is a hub of traffic of the center and the huge screens with ads liken the Piccadilly Square with the New York Times. With modern style but with classic references, the restaurant "The Dream" proposes a range of modern dishes tonnes, with the signature of executive chef David Burke.

The style of the restaurant's dishes match the overall aesthetics of the room, where modern style prevails but without overwhelming the classic that "rooting out" from anywhere. Feature of the restaurant is the glass floor, through which one can see impressive ancient finds. The atmosphere combined with the aromas of herbs and spices create a place that promises a wonderful culinary adventure to its guests.
